Find Me Uni

uni-for-you.herokuapp.com

Find Me Uni helps potential college students find the right university for them.

After creating a personalized account, students log in, and then enter in their SAT and ACT scores. From there, using an external API, the student will receive a list of schools that will compare their test scores against the average accepted rating.

Technologies used include:

  • HTML & CSS,
  • React.js,
  • Node.js,
  • AJAX,
  • MongoDB
